Personal wellness

Seek Skills

Are you acquiring skills or merely collecting certificates?

The true value lies in what we can do,not in what you can prove on paper.Imagine you're an employer:would you prefer an employee with a certificate or one with demonstrated skills and competencies?

Seek Knowledge,Not Accreditation

How deeply do you understand the subject you,ve studied?

Strive to understand concepts deeply and apply them practically.A mind enriched with knowledge is far more valuable that one laded with titles.

Seek Guidance and Mentorship

Who are the mentors in your life?

Seek mentors who can provide wisdom and insights that accelerate your growth and help you avoid common pitfalls

Seek Networks,not Friends with Benefits

How strong and supportive is your current network?

Network provide support,opportunities,and resources that are essential for personal and professional growth.Focus on building genuine connections rather than superficial friendship based on mutual benefits.

Seek Meaningful Relationships, Not Sexual Acquaintances

Are your current relationships meaningful and fulfilling?

In an era where relationships can often be superficial, strive to build meaningful connections. These relationships enrich your life, provide emotional support, and help you grow as a person.

Seek Knowledge from Books, Not Just Social Media

How often do you read books compared to browsing social media?

Social media is a double-edged sword. While it offers vast amounts of information, it also comes with distractions. Books, on the other hand, offer depth and focus. Make reading a habit to gain deeper insights and knowledge.

FOUNDER/DIRECTOR

Kajiado Digital Innovators(KDI)

I run Kajiado Digital Innovators (KDI) a community-based organization in Kajiado Town, Ildamat Ward, Kajiado County. KDI is devoted to empowering individuals by teaching valuable digital skills that lead to job opportunities. The organization also uses technology to fight gender-based violence and climate change, reduce drug abuse, and provide meals for vulnerable community members. KDI supports mental health through technology and is dedicated to donating sanitary towels to those in need.

  • Empowering individuals with valuable digital skills for enhanced job opportunities.
  • Utilizing technology and educational programs to fight gender-based violence.
  • Implementing technology-driven initiatives to mitigate climate change.
  • Engaging the community with tech-based solutions and educational campaigns to combat drug abuse.
  • Organizing meal programs and distributing food to vulnerable community members.
  • Using technology to offer mental health support and resources.
